PontosGratis Documentation

Pontos.Gratis Referral Program#

User Advocacy Program#

The referral system encourages users to act as advocates, helping to onboard businesses and spread awareness about the platform. Users earn PontosReais for referring businesses, creating social media content, getting friends to install the app, and referring businesses to friends.

Business Referral Program#

Onboarding Reward#

Users receive 30% of the business onboarding fee in PontosReais when they successfully refer a business to the platform.

Milestones#

After successfully referring 10 businesses, users unlock additional PontosReais rewards or exclusive perks, such as limited-edition NFTs or special recognition within the platform.

Social Media Engagement#

Users earn PontosReais for creating and sharing social media content about Pontos.Gratis, helping increase platform visibility. Content can include videos, posts, or stories showcasing their experience with the platform or specific businesses.

Friend Referral Program#

App Referrals#

Users who refer friends to download and sign up for the app will earn PontosReais once their friends complete the registration process and make their first transaction on the platform.

Business Referrals to Friends#

Users can refer specific businesses to their friends through the app. When a friend visits and makes a purchase at that business, both the referrer and the referred friend receive PontosReais rewards.

Geographical Leaderboards & Region-Based Features#

Regional Leaderboards#

Users who refer the most businesses in a specific geographic region are crowned Regional Leaders, earning extra PontosReais, rare NFTs, or other exclusive perks.

Local Check-ins#

Users earn additional PontosReais by checking into partner businesses and making purchases.

Regional Challenges#

Competitions within a geographic area to onboard businesses or generate engagement.

Verified Review System#

Receipt-Based Reviews with Photos#

After scanning a receipt, users can leave verified reviews for businesses or specific products. They can upload pictures of the business, their purchases, or products they enjoyed. These reviews, combined with real photos, make the feedback more credible and engaging.

Bonus for Specific Items#

If a business promotes a specific product (e.g., a new menu item), users can scan their receipt showing the purchase of that item and leave a review with photos. In return, they can earn additional PontosReais or special promotional points from the business.

Gamification Elements#

Achievement Badges#

Users earn badges for completing specific referral or engagement milestones (e.g., “10 Business Referrals,” “Top Reviewer,” “Social Media Influencer”). These badges will be displayed on user profiles and provide status recognition within the community.

Referral Streaks#

Users can earn extra PontosReais through streak bonuses by consecutively referring businesses or friends over a set time period (e.g., refer a business every week for a month and earn a streak multiplier).

NFT Collectibles as Rewards#

For top performers in regional leaderboards, referral streaks, or social media engagement, issue limited-edition NFTs that represent their contribution or advocacy. These NFTs will be exclusive, rare, and may hold value within the Pontos.Gratis ecosystem.

Additional Incentives#

Photo Reviews and Social Shares#

Users are incentivized to take photos of the business or products they purchase. This populates the app with user-generated content, making the business pages visually appealing. Reviews with photos will earn users additional PontosReais, and users can share their reviews on social media for even more rewards.

Leaderboard Competitions#

Monthly rewards for the top performers across friend referrals, business onboarding, and social media engagement.

Location-Based Events#

Special rewards for attending physical events or engaging with businesses in person through the app.
