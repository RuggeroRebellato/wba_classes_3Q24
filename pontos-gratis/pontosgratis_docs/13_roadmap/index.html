Pontos.Gratis Roadmap: Product Development & Business Growth (2024-2026)#

Development Roadmap Overview#

This roadmap outlines the key product development milestones and business growth targets for Pontos.Gratis from the MVP launch through the Brazilian market expansion in 2026. It aligns with the realistic growth expectations based on the Brazilian SME and retail market.

2024: Q4 (MVP Launch)#

Product Deliveries:#

  • Custom Token Creation: Allows businesses to create their own loyalty tokens.
  • Basic Loyalty Programs: Implements simple token-based rewards.
  • Referral System: User and business referrals earn rewards in PontosReais.
  • Geolocation Rewards: Early version of location-based promotions.
  • Fiat-to-Crypto Onramp: Enables fiat purchases of tokens via integrated payment providers.
  • Basic Analytics Dashboard: Tracks key metrics like redemptions and referrals.

Business Projections:#

  • Businesses Onboarded: 2,000 businesses.
  • MAUs: 50,000 users.
  • Revenue: Onboarding fees generate $100,000 by year-end.

2025: Q1-Q2 (Expansion in Local Markets)#

Product Deliveries:#

  • Enhanced Referral System: Optimized referral features for businesses and users.
  • Localized Promotions: Region-specific campaigns to drive local engagement.
  • Push Notification Campaigns: Geo-targeted offers for nearby users.

Business Projections:#

  • Businesses Onboarded: Additional 3,000 businesses (total: 5,000).
  • MAUs: 150,000 users.
  • Revenue: Onboarding fees and transaction fees boost revenue to $250,000.

2025: Q3 (Advanced Analytics Rollout)#

Product Deliveries:#

  • Advanced Analytics Dashboard: Predictive insights like churn prediction and CLV.
  • A/B Testing Tools: Allows businesses to experiment with loyalty campaigns.
  • Custom Analytics & Insights: Paid access to detailed user behavior reports.

Business Projections:#

  • Businesses Onboarded: Additional 2,500 businesses (total: 7,500).
  • MAUs: 250,000 users.
  • Revenue: Advanced analytics subscriptions contribute $1 million annually.

2025: Q4 (Gamification & NFT Integration)#

Product Deliveries:#

  • Leaderboards & Badges: Gamified user engagement through achievements.
  • NFT Challenges: Businesses reward customers with NFTs for loyalty milestones.
  • NFT Marketplace: Allows users to trade and showcase earned NFTs.

Business Projections:#

  • Businesses Onboarded: Additional 2,500 businesses (total: 10,000).
  • MAUs: 300,000 users.
  • Revenue: NFT challenges and marketplace transactions generate $1.5 million annually.

2026: Q1-Q2 (Full Brazilian Market Expansion)#

Product Deliveries:#

  • PontosReais Liquidity Pools: Businesses and users can participate in liquidity pools and earn rewards.
  • Fees on PontosReais Trading: 0.5% fee on token swaps and liquidity pool participation.
  • Advanced Custom Loyalty Programs: Full customization for businesses to design dynamic loyalty systems.

Business Projections:#

  • Businesses Onboarded: Additional 5,000 businesses (total: 15,000).
  • MAUs: 350,000 users.
  • Revenue: PontosReais liquidity pool fees contribute $2 million annually.

2026: Q3-Q4 (Scaling Revenue Streams)#

Product Deliveries:#

  • Paid Ads & Promotions Platform: Businesses can purchase ads and highlighted spots on the app to reach users.
  • Full Analytics Rollout: Comprehensive reporting and insights available via subscription plans.

Business Projections:#

  • Businesses Onboarded: Additional 5,000 businesses (total: 20,000).
  • MAUs: 400,000 users.
  • Revenue: Paid ads generate $10 million in revenue, with analytics subscriptions adding $2.5 million annually. Total annual revenue reaches $18.5 million.

Final Business Projections for 2026:#

  • Total Businesses Onboarded: 20,000.
  • Total MAUs: 400,000.
  • Total Annual Revenue: $18.5 million, driven by:
    • Transaction Fees (PontosReais and token trades): $3.6 million.
    • Business Subscriptions (Analytics, Ads, A/B Testing): $2.5 million.
    • Paid Ads & Promotions: $10 million.
    • Fees on PontosReais Liquidity Pools: $2.4 million.
