PontosGratis Documentation

User App Overview#

The Pontos.Gratis user app serves as the central hub for managing PONTOS, tracking rewards, engaging with promotions, interacting with custom tokens, and unlocking NFTs. Designed with a minimalist, neobrutalist aesthetic, the app focuses on usability, clarity, and seamless interaction for both crypto-savvy and non-crypto users.

Blockchain’s growing presence in loyalty programs promises increased user engagement and reduced system inefficiencies. By leveraging blockchain, loyalty systems can cut operational costs by up to 30% while reducing fraud and boosting transparency. This shift is essential for Pontos.Gratis’ scalable growth.

Key Features#

1. Manage PONTOS and Custom Tokens#

  • Token Balances: Users can view their PONTOS, business-specific tokens, and the equivalent BRL value in their dashboard. The app also shows real-time updates on token balances.
  • Token Swaps: One-click functionality allows users to convert business tokens into PontosReais via liquidity pools. The seamless conversion experience eliminates the complexities of traditional loyalty programs.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Instant updates on token conversions provide immediate visual feedback, making the app dynamic and responsive.

2. Track Rewards and Milestones#

  • Reward Overview: Users see their accumulated rewards sorted by business or token type, with a dynamic progress bar showing their journey toward the next milestone.
  • Dynamic Milestones: The app automatically adjusts personal milestones based on user activity, creating personalized goals.
  • Notification System: Users receive alerts for milestones, upcoming promotions, and completed achievements, enhancing engagement.

3. Engage with Promotions and Local Businesses#

  • Geolocation Features: Users earn bonus tokens by checking into partner businesses and participating in local challenges, leveraging blockchain’s capacity for instant validation.
  • Cross-Promotions: Users can engage in promotions across multiple businesses for extra rewards, with suggestions tailored to their shopping habits.
  • Active Promotions: Easily accessible promotions provide opportunities for users to unlock extra rewards through special offers and campaigns.

4. NFTs and Gamification#

  • NFT Rewards: Users can unlock exclusive NFTs by achieving milestones, completing challenges, or maintaining loyalty streaks. Blockchain’s transparency ensures secure ownership and exchange of NFTs.
  • Achievement Badges: Badges reward users for their accomplishments, displayed prominently on their profiles.
  • Leaderboards: Regional leaderboards highlight top users based on referrals, participation, and milestones, adding a competitive element.

5. Fiat to Crypto Onramping with Pix#

  • Pix Integration: Users can convert BRL into PontosReais directly in the app using the Pix payment system, ensuring a fast and secure process without requiring third-party exchanges. Blockchain’s efficiency in processing and securing transactions supports a seamless conversion.
  • Simplified Conversion: Instant feedback ensures users know when their PontosReais are ready for use in the loyalty ecosystem.

6. Referral System and Social Engagement#

  • Referral Tracking: Users can track their referral progress and receive notifications when referred friends or businesses complete their first transactions.
  • Business Referrals: Rewards escalate as users refer more businesses, unlocking higher-level rewards such as NFTs or bonus PontosReais.
  • Social Media Engagement: Users earn additional rewards for sharing their experiences on social media, increasing both visibility and user participation.

Real-World Scenario: Maria’s Journey Through the App#

Maria, a small business owner in São Paulo, downloaded the Pontos.Gratis app after hearing about it from a friend. Here’s her journey:

  1. Onboarding:

    • Maria quickly signed up with her email and was introduced to the app’s features. She easily purchased PontosReais using the Pix integration, bridging her familiar payment method with the crypto world.

  2. First Purchase:

    • Maria shopped at ZipZap Supermercados, earning ZapCoins by scanning her NFC-e receipt through the app.
    • She tracked her growing ZapCoins balance and, with a single click, swapped them for PontosReais, investing the rest in ZipZap’s liquidity pool.

  3. Tracking Rewards:

    • Maria set a personal milestone to earn 10,000 PONTOS in a month, and the app showed her progress in real-time. She checked active promotions at ZipZap and maximized her bonus by shopping during an active promotion.

  4. Referrals:

    • Maria shared her success on social media and referred two friends. She earned PontosReais when they made their first purchases, keeping her motivated to share more.

  5. Unlocking NFTs:

    • After reaching her milestone, Maria unlocked an exclusive ZipZap NFT for her loyalty, proudly displaying it in her app.

User Experience and Interface#

The app leverages a neobrutalist design, with bold typography, oversized buttons, and clear, high-contrast visuals to guide users through key actions. Blockchain’s ability to handle complex, secure transactions makes the app both scalable and efficient.

  • Quick Actions Bar: Core functions like Swap Tokens and Check In are easily accessible, minimizing user friction.
  • Interactive Feedback: Subtle animations provide instant gratification after completing actions like reaching milestones or referring friends.
  • Minimalist Navigation: Prioritized core actions ensure a frictionless user experience across all interactions.

Conclusion#

The Pontos.Gratis user app offers a seamless experience for users to manage their PONTOS, engage with promotions, and explore a new world of decentralized rewards. Blockchain’s integration enhances security, transparency, and scalability while reducing operational inefficiencies by 30%. The app’s intuitive design and gamification features ensure long-term user engagement, making it a powerful tool in the evolving loyalty program landscape.
