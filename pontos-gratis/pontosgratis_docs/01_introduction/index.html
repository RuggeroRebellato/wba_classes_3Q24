Pontos.Gratis: A Blockchain-Powered Loyalty System for Businesses and Consumers#

Pontos.Gratis is a decentralized loyalty and rewards platform designed to bridge the gap between local businesses and consumers. By leveraging blockchain technology, Pontos.Gratis offers a seamless way for businesses to create custom loyalty programs, and for consumers to easily earn and redeem rewards.

What is Pontos.Gratis?#

At its core, Pontos.Gratis transforms everyday purchases into an accessible crypto experience. Through features like NFC-e receipt scanning, custom tokens, and the use of PontosReais—a stablecoin pegged to the Brazilian Real—the platform empowers businesses to engage customers while offering users the ability to earn, trade, and redeem rewards across multiple businesses.

Key Features:#

  • NFC-e Receipt Scanning: Consumers earn rewards by scanning purchase receipts, verified automatically through Brazil’s tax system.
  • Custom Tokens for Businesses: Businesses can create their own tokens for loyalty programs, allowing users to accumulate and exchange points in a decentralized system.
  • PontosReais (Stablecoin): A stablecoin pegged to the Brazilian Real, enabling users to redeem business tokens and swap them for rewards or cash.
  • Business Dashboard: A management portal where businesses can create promotions, manage token values, and track customer engagement.

Why Pontos.Gratis?#

Pontos.Gratis is designed to simplify the adoption of blockchain for local businesses, allowing them to leverage crypto technology without the complexity. It also offers consumers a new way to interact with businesses, earning tangible rewards that can be easily traded or redeemed in real life.
